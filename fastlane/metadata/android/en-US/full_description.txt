<p><i>Moneytopia</i> is a fully functional app for tracking personal expenses, with a simple UI for easy understanding and ease of use.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>Material 3 (Material You) UI with Dynamic Color</li><li>Automatic light/dark mode</li><li>Multi-language (currently available: en-US, id-ID)</li><li>Recent transactions within the last 24 hours</li><li>A report that contains all expenses</li><li>Expenses in a line chart view</li><li>Swipe-able action to display expenses from a certain period of time</li><li>Delete all expense records or delete them one by one, and</li><li>No unnecessary permissions needed to use this app</li></ul>
